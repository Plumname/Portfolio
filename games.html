Project 01

Long Way Up

2D Shoot´em up / Rouguelite

Once a guardian of the sky, the mighty dragon, Long, has fallen - Help him fight back against his demonic captors and reach the sky once again.

Unity Clip Studio Paint
About

This game was made by a 4 person team for a university assignment. The goal was to make a roguelike/-lite game. In Long Way Up, Players play as Long - a dragon guardian who has been improsined in the pits of hell. His demon captures want to restrain the guardian of the land, so they can bring chaos upon it. The game starts with Long escaping his prison and flying up to reach his home - the sky. On his way, he has to fight enemies with his fire attack, dodge obstacles and search fpr upgrades that strengthen his powers. The updates are purchasable by collecting coins that are droppe by the enemy. Every time you die or when passing the level, you can access the shop. There are two different attack types unlockable and the chance of healing. I made the art, game concept and all animations - including the ui

Features
  • Hand drawn assets
  • 4 Playable Levels including Boss fight
  • Permanent aquirable updates
  • Multiple Attack modes
  • Final 2D animated cutscene
